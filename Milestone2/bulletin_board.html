Bulletin Broad


Volunteers are required!! For us to promote live performances, we have to reduce our production costs. We are assisted by the Townsville City Counsil which allows us to do a number of tasks by our volunteers. Most of the volunteer activities do not require any specific skills such as setting out or stacking chairs.


Ushers play a critial part by taking tickets at the door and ensuring that all patrons are comfortable and safe. A short, free training session will be assential for this part, to learn the theatre policies, familiarising about emergency evacuation procedures etc. Ushers are then signed up with the Civic Theatre management where they work at many theatre performances throughout the year.

Please call: 4724 2086 if you are interested in taking part in these rewarding activities.

Our Centre

Townsville Community Music Centre is a not-for-profit, incorporated organisation serving musicians and their audience in North Queensland. The Music Centre's role includes facilitation, presentation and promotion of events, functions, education, workshops, concerts, festivals in Townsville, and surrounding centres.
